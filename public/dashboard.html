How Bitfreeze Works

📝

1. Register & Verify

Create your free Bitfreeze account using your email and M-Pesa phone number. Your account is secured and verified instantly.

🧊

2. Choose a Fridge

Browse our range of earning fridges — from KES 100 starters to KES 8,000 premium fridges. Each fridge earns you a fixed daily income.

💰

3. Pay via M-Pesa Till

Pay using M-Pesa Buy Goods — Till Number 6992349. Submit your transaction code and wait for admin approval.

📈

4. Earn Daily

After 24 hours your fridge starts earning. Daily earnings are credited to your account every night at 12:00 AM Kenya time automatically.

🎁

5. Grab Special Offers

Admin unlocks special Offer Fridges for a limited time. These give you a higher total return over a short period — grab them fast!

💸

6. Withdraw Anytime

Withdraw your earnings to your M-Pesa number anytime. Minimum withdrawal is KES 200. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bitfreeze safe to use? ▼
Yes. Bitfreeze uses secure JWT authentication, encrypted passwords, and all payments are manually verified by our admin team before any fridge is activated.
When do I start earning? ▼
You start earning 24 hours after your fridge purchase is approved by admin. Earnings are then credited to your account every night at midnight Kenya time.
How do I pay for a fridge? ▼
Go to M-Pesa → Lipa na M-Pesa → Buy Goods & Services → Enter Till Number 6992349 → Enter the exact fridge price → Submit your M-Pesa transaction code in the app.
How long does approval take? ▼
Payments are typically approved within a few hours. Our admin team verifies every M-Pesa code manually to protect all users from fraud.
Can I own multiple fridges? ▼
Yes! You can own as many fridges as you like. Each fridge earns independently, so more fridges means more daily income.
What are Offer Fridges? ▼
Offer Fridges are special limited-time fridges unlocked by the admin. They run for a set number of hours and pay out their full total earning to your balance the moment the timer ends.
What is the minimum withdrawal? ▼
The minimum withdrawal amount is KES 200. Your withdrawal phone number must match the phone number registered on your account.
I submitted the wrong M-Pesa code. What do I do? ▼
Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or Telegram with your correct transaction code. Our admin will update your payment record.

About Bitfreeze

Our Story

Bitfreeze was founded in 2024 in Nairobi, Kenya, with a simple mission — give everyday Kenyans access to a simple, transparent, and reliable daily earning platform.

We noticed that most investment platforms were too complicated, required large capital, or weren't built for the Kenyan market. Bitfreeze was built differently — using M-Pesa, the payment system Kenyans already trust, and keeping the process as simple as buying goods at a shop.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyans with a simple passive income stream. Whether you invest KES 100 or KES 8,000 — Bitfreeze works for you every single day.

Why "Bitfreeze"?

Just like a fridge preserves and grows value over time, Bitfreeze preserves your money and grows it daily. The "Bit" represents the digital nature of our platform — modern, fast, and always available.

Our Values

🔒 Transparency — Every payment is manually verified. No hidden fees.
⚡ Speed — Fast approvals, instant earnings at midnight.
🤝 Trust — We've served hundreds of Kenyan users since launch.
📱 Accessibility — Works on any phone, anywhere in Kenya.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and a resident of Kenya to use Bitfreeze. You must provide a valid M-Pesa registered phone number.

2. Payments

All payments are made via M-Pesa Buy Goods Till Number 6992349. Bitfreeze is not responsible for payments sent to the wrong till number. Always verify the till number before paying.

3. Earnings

Daily earnings are credited every night at 12:00 AM Kenya time for normal fridges. Offer fridge earnings are credited immediately when the offer timer ends. Earnings are estimates based on fridge type and may not be guaranteed in extraordinary circumstances.

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. Bitfreeze will never ask for your password. Report any suspicious activity immediately.
