How Bitfreeze Works

📝

1. Register & Verify

Create your free Bitfreeze account using your email and M-Pesa phone number. Your account is secured and verified instantly.

🥶

2. Choose a Fridge

Browse our range of earning fridges — from KES 100 starters to KES 8,000 premium fridges. Each fridge earns you a fixed daily income.

💰

3. Pay via M-Pesa Paybill

Go to M-Pesa → Lipa na M-Pesa → Pay Bill. Enter Business No. 222111, Account No. 3037819, and the exact fridge amount. Paste your M-Pesa confirmation SMS in the app to submit for approval.

📈

4. Earn Daily

After 24 hours your fridge starts earning. Daily earnings are credited to your account every night at 12:00 AM Kenya time automatically.

🎁

5. Grab Special Offers

Admin unlocks special Offer Fridges for a limited time. These give you a higher total return over a short period — grab them fast!

💸

6. Withdraw Anytime

Withdraw your earnings to your M-Pesa number anytime. Minimum withdrawal is KES 200. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bitfreeze safe to use? ▼
Yes. Bitfreeze uses secure JWT authentication, encrypted passwords, and all payments are verified against your M-Pesa confirmation message before any fridge is activated.
When do I start earning? ▼
You start earning 24 hours after your fridge purchase is approved by admin. Earnings are then credited to your account every night at midnight Kenya time.
How do I pay for a fridge? ▼
Click Buy on any fridge. Go to M-Pesa → Lipa na M-Pesa → Pay Bill. Enter Business No. 222111 and Account No. 3037819. Enter the exact fridge price and your PIN. Copy the full M-Pesa confirmation SMS and paste it into the app, then tap Submit for Approval.
How long does approval take? ▼
Payments are typically approved within a few hours. Our team verifies every M-Pesa confirmation message to protect all users. Once approved, your fridge starts earning within 24 hours.
Can I own multiple fridges? ▼
Yes! You can own as many fridges as you like. Each fridge earns independently, so more fridges means more daily income.
What are Offer Fridges? ▼
Offer Fridges are special limited-time fridges unlocked by the admin. They run for a set number of hours and pay out their full total earning to your balance the moment the timer ends.
What is the minimum withdrawal? ▼
The minimum withdrawal amount is KES 200. Your withdrawal phone number must match the phone number registered on your account.
I sent to the wrong paybill. What do I do? ▼
Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or Telegram with your M-Pesa confirmation message. Our admin will update your payment record as quickly as possible.

About Bitfreeze

Our Story

Bitfreeze was founded in 2024 in Nairobi, Kenya, with a simple mission — give everyday Kenyans access to a simple, transparent, and reliable daily earning platform.

We noticed that most investment platforms were too complicated, required large capital, or weren't built for the Kenyan market. Bitfreeze was built differently — using M-Pesa, the payment system Kenyans already trust.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyans with a simple passive income stream. Whether you invest KES 100 or KES 8,000 — Bitfreeze works for you every single day.

Why "Bitfreeze"?

Just like a fridge preserves and grows value over time, Bitfreeze preserves your money and grows it daily. The "Bit" represents the digital nature of our platform — modern, fast, and always available.

Our Values

🔒 Transparency — Every payment is verified before activation. No hidden fees.
⚡ Speed — Fast approvals, instant earnings at midnight.
🤝 Trust — We've served hundreds of Kenyan users since launch.
📱 Accessibility — Works on any phone, anywhere in Kenya.

Terms & Conditions

By using Bitfreeze, you agree to the following terms. Please read carefully.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and a resident of Kenya to use Bitfreeze. You must provide a valid M-Pesa registered phone number.

2. Payments

All payments are made via M-Pesa Paybill — Business Number 222111, Account Number 3037819. You must paste your M-Pesa confirmation SMS in the app after paying. Bitfreeze is not responsible for payments sent to the wrong paybill number or using the wrong account number.

3. Earnings

Daily earnings are credited every night at 12:00 AM Kenya time for normal fridges. Offer fridge earnings are credited immediately when the offer timer ends.

4. Withdrawals

Minimum withdrawal is KES 200. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Your withdrawal phone must match your registered account phone number.

5. Account Security

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. Bitfreeze will never ask for your password. Report any suspicious activity immediately.

6. Prohibited Activities

Submitting fake M-Pesa confirmation messages, creating multiple accounts, or attempting to manipulate the platform in any way will result in permanent account suspension.

Bitfreeze reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
